NOW is a privately held, closed corporation. Therefore, the shares are not available to the public. To invest in this dynamic company, potential investors must be friends or family of a NOW Inc. Board Member or Shareholder. Other interested investors are encouraged to submit a formal inquiry to the company.

Mission – NOW develops wealth through strategic investments for our families.

Vision - NOW is the premier provider of multi-generational wealth opportunities and financial education for our investors and stakeholders.

“Let your money work for you” - Investing in NOW offers shareholders an opportunity to generate passive income that builds multi-generational wealth, while also investing in our communities. As a friend or family member, you are receiving first access to this wealth building opportunity.
